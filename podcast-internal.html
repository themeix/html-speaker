Podcast The Digital Speaker

The Digital Speaker talks about the future of work and the organisation of tomorrow.

The Digital Speaker is here to tell you about the ins and outs of emerging digital technologies and how this will affect your business and society, from inside the digital world. Over the past 30-years, our lives have been increasingly digitalised as we live through the third industrial revolution. From the moment we wake up to the moment we close our eyes, the digital world surrounds us. From AI and Quantum Computing through to Blockchain and Cryptocurrency, The Tech Journal will explore digital innovations, looking at what they are, what they do, and if they’ll help or hinder our lives Join me on the Tech Journal for your digital download.
